FAQs:

  • Is the move-in date flexible? How do I move?
    The move-in date may be flexible - this can be discussed. When the date is decided, we can coordinate in advance with building management. They require a $200 deposit to reserve the service elevator (returned after move-in is complete) and a $150 non-refundable move-in fee.
  • How much does parking cost?
    The attached garage is operated by State Parking. At the time of this writing (February 2020) the monthly cost for residents is $260. More information is available here. Zoned street parking is also available.
  • Can I smoke? How about pets?
    No smoking or pets allowed in the unit or in the building, sorry!
  • Can I sign a lease longer/shorter than 12 months?
    The building requires an initial lease of at least 12 months. After the first year, there is more flexibility. We are happy to discuss 18-to-24 month leases if preferred.
  • Do I need to pay a security deposit?
    A security deposit equal to one month of rent is required. At the end of the lease, in accordance with City of Chicago laws, this deposit, plus interest, minus unpaid rent and expenses for damages, will be returned.
